Who am I?

Who am I ? My name is Jalen Jamari Durden! I'm just a guy trying to get out of the food industry and dip my toes into new things.

I think Tech and more specifically Web Development would be very interesting to get into. I've talked to different people about the demands of this stuff and the pros and cons of this industry and it seems manageable to me.

Ideally, I'd like to do this as remote work, so that I can travel, live another country, and not do much more physical labor than I have to. Also, would like to be more at home and not have to commute somwhere. All in all, working remotely as a web developer would free me up to do a lot more things and have more time to do the things I need and want. And a little more money wouldn't hurt!
